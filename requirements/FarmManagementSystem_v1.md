# Farm Management System - Version 1

## Project Goal

Build a personal farm management system for agricultural operations.

Primary objectives:

- Track lands
- Track crops
- Track expenses
- Track harvests
- Track income
- Track cattle
- Track tractor expenses
- Maintain dealer and buyer information

Future versions may include:

- Reports
- Charts
- Calendar reminders
- GPS maps
- AI chatbot
- Voice note entry
- OCR/image recognition


## Modules

### Land Management

Store:

- Land Name
- Survey Number
- Patta Number
- Area (Acres)

### Crop Management

Supported crops:

- Coconut
- Sugarcane
- Turmeric
- Sesame
- Cassava
- Onion
- Fodder Corn

### Cattle Management

- Feed expenses
- Milk production
- Milk sales

### Tractor Management

- Fuel expenses
- Service expenses
- Repair expenses
- Tyre replacement
- Battery replacement
